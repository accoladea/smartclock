Smart Clock

Purpose:

The purpose of SmartClock is to allow small business employers an efficient way to track and manage their employees schedules. It offers a streamlined management system of hourly rates and assignment of shifts, while calculating a more precise time log of the exact hours spent working.

Features:

- Easy login and registration
- Allows easy clock in and clock out
- Securely hashes passwords for employee protection
- Allows employers to manage hourly rates for employees

- View and manage user profiles
- Offers more precise salary calculation
- Overtime pay calculation
- Employer approval system for reported hours
